About IDLE




The project IDLE is aiming to provide the current availability of a location by showing a heat map of parking spaces, public facilities and restaurant. The IDLE demo project will operate checking availability of public facilities like benches and toilet, it's also ready to be powered up by new 5G networks for more functions.
